Albers-o-Matic

Cooper Union, Foundation Year Prompts, Color, Processing, Coding, Meme Generator, New York

A simple program, written in Processing, that generates compositions in the same geometric style as Joseph Albers, the famous color theorist. Whereas Albers’ compositions are carefully considered color studies, these color palettes are completely randomly generated. Can you tell the difference between Albers and Albers-O-Matic?
