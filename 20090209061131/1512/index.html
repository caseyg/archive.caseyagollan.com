About This Project
In fall 2006, I was cleaning out my closet when I found a bag of about 10 rolls of exposed film. I had forgotten about the film, but remembered that I shot it the summer before while visiting my family in Scandinavia and Virginia. For my Photo II concentration, I carefully printed and curated a selection of these photos for my final exhibit.

My photography is informal, personal, and documentary. Rather than fine art, I consider it my journal, as much of it is sentimental. I am constantly taking photos which you can follow on my flickr account.
