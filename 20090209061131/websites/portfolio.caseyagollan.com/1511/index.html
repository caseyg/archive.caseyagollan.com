About this Project
I created these unfired ceramic experiments at Rye High School from January through May of 2008. After amassing a large amount of these experiments, I documented the most successful pieces and then shoved them off the table into the garbage can. It was incredibly liberating to work in ceramics but have the final form be photography. The pieces were not bound by the limitations of ceramics, where the final form is an object that needs to be fired, glazed, or of archival quality.
