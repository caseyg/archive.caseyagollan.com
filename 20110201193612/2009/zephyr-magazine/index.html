by Casey A. Gollan

Along with a team of some of the most amazing people I've ever met, I designed and edited this magazine for four years.

Instead of a boring old linear Table of Contents, I thought it would be fun to try designing Zephyr's as an infographic. The length of each bar corresponds to the word count of the piece of writing and the color of the bar represents the average color of the nearest piece of art.

It ended up being a useful way to find what you're looking for, but also to get lost in the magazine's contents. Awarded 1st place in Infographics for 2009 from Columbia University Scholastic Press Association.

We won five other awards from the Columbia University Scholastic Press Association for this issue!
1st and 3rd place for various page designs
2nd place for overall design
2nd place for color photography
Honorable mention for poetry
