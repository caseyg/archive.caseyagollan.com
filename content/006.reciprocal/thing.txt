title: Reciprocal
-
date: 2007 Fall
-
excerpt: A project stemming from the word &lquo;reciprocal.&rdquo; I placed white sheets of paper in front of active doorways to capture aggregate footsteps.
-
tags: Oxbow School, Installation, Napa, California
-
content: From here I became interested in how people move in set routes. For example, you can find your way around your house with your eyes closed. Even on these mats you can see how people enter and exit buildings at similar and consistent angles.
